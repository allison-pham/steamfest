What is STEAM Fest?

STEAM Fest is an extraordinary celebration tailored for high school and college students. Spanning two dynamic weekends in June, STEAM Fest promises an immersive experience with 24 captivating events. The excitement is extended over the two weeks during June 10th to 23rd, where thrilling competitions and engaging trivia rounds await! Not only do participants have the chance to showcase their skills, but they also stand to win enticing prizes generously provided by our sponsors.As we gear up for the second year of STEAM Fest, let's reflect on last year's triumph—a resounding success with more than 100 enthusiastic registrants and three exhilarating competitions that left participants inspired and invigorated. Don't miss out on this year's STEAM Fest—it's bound to be bigger, better, and more unforgettable than ever before!
