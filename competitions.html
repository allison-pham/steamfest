Competitions

STEAM Fest features 4 exciting competitions. Don't miss your chance to join in!

Illustrate a Quote

June 10, 2024 to June 20, 2024

The STEAM Boat invites participants to participate in Illustrate a Quote. Participants will choose or make up their own quote and use any form of art media to display this quote. It can include drawing, painting, online art, etc. Any form of AI usage or plagiarism will result in disqualification. The quote can be from a song, poem, prose, book, or it can be made up. Participants can also use their old work that correlates with a quote. Participants will be evaluated based on quality of art, accuracy of depiction, originality, and creativity. Winners will receive an Instagram post feature and a certificate.

Submit Entry

Environmental Sustainability Video Competition

June 10, 2024 to June 18, 2024

The Environmental Sustainability Video Competition invites individuals and groups to produce a 2-minute video that educates and inspires the audience on topics related to environmental sustainability. Whether it's showcasing the benefits of renewable energy, strategies for waste reduction, the importance of biodiversity conservation, or other crucial aspects of environmental stewardship, the goal is to raise awareness and drive action towards a sustainable future.

Submit Entry

Article Craft Challenge

June 10, 2024 to June 17, 2024

Participants must submit an article about any STEM topic that interests them. This will be a non-fiction (factual) piece of work. Participants will be judged on grammar, bias, credibility, structure, and more.

Submit Entry

College/Career Prep Bingo

June 10, 2024 to June 23, 2024

Participants will complete 9 tasks related to either college prep or post-grad (getting a job) prep. Which card to complete will be determined by whether they are in high school or not. For college prep, tasks range from attending events at TSB that cover the topic to going on a college visit. For post-grad prep, tasks range from attending events at TSB that cover the topic to creating and refining their LinkedIn account. We hope to be able to give a prize to everyone who completes a card. If we do not have enough prizes, we will do a drawing.
